CO₂ reduction & Countering climate change

Climate change is one of the greatest challenges that we as humanity should not neglect. The earth is heating up and that has consequences for the climate. The greenhouse effect is amplified by the amount of greenhouse gases in the air. Global warming cannot be stopped, but things will go less fast if we emit less CO2 worldwide.

Global warming cannot be stopped. Due to the amount of greenhouse gas that man has already emitted, it will get warmer this century. How much the earth will warm depends on what measures are taken worldwide. But we at CO2naarbenee want to reduce this effect by supporting charities with your donations and thereby helping to slow down climate change
