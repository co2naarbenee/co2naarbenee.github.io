Goal of this project

What does the project entail?

We want to reduce greenhouse gas emissions with the help of your donations. Every euro is donated to charity in your name. A percentage of the money raised goes to nature preservation foundations and tree planting organizations, the rest goes to local, smaller charities.

We will notify you where the money will be donated on the Our goals page. For direct questions about our promotion, please visit the social media section at the bottom of the page.

Next

Which foundations do we support?

We support many nature funds with this project, such as:

  • Cool Earth
  • The Sierra Club: Beyond Coal
  • SolarAid
  • Sandbag

In addition to these funds, we also support charities that have the same cause as us. Only together can we ensure that the earth remains livable for future generations.
